Native app development is the process of creating software applications that are specifically designed to be used on a particular platform or device, such as a smartphone or tablet. Native apps offer a number of benefits over web-based or hybrid apps, including faster performance, offline functionality, and access to device-specific features and capabilities. If you're a developer looking to create a high-quality, seamless user experience for a specific platform, native app development is the way to go.
